INTRO

I come from a small town, Contai, near the coast of Bay of Bengal, on Planet Earth. I completed my high school education from Contai Public School. And then moved to Kolkata for my University Education. Since my childhood, I am facinated by the stars, planets and moons. Being always wished to be an Intersteller traveler and greatly inspired by the movie "Interstellar", my primary field of Research is on Extra-solar planets. Search for Habitable Planets is one of my greatest goal. Inspiration can come form anywhere and for me, its Sci-Fi movies. I am one of the biggest consumer of Sci-Fi movies. Apart form this, I love cycling and travelling to new places (well in my language I say, "Exoploring Earth!").
