From mixing moons and stars in milk to studying the planets and stars in the Milky Way, this is how my jouney started. I have a keen interest in Physics, especially in the field of Astrophysics. I love to work with huge amounts of data, to study and extract meaningful insights for analysis. I have such a huge enthusiasm about outer space, planets, asteroids and meteors, the topics that fascinate me more are exoplanets and star and planet formation. Currently, working on projects related to exoplanet detection, analysis and atmospheric spectroscopy. All of the current projects mostly include analysing data gathered from space and ground- based telescopes and also simulating data using various software packages and theoretical study.
